**10 AI Prompts to Automate Client Onboarding & Reporting**

**Prompt 1: Personalized Client Welcome Email**

**Purpose:** Generate a warm, professional, and personalized welcome email for a new client, outlining initial next steps.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Draft a personalized welcome email for a new client, [Client Company Name], for our [Service Type] service.
Include:
1. A warm welcome and expression of excitement to work with them.
2. Confirmation of the service they've signed up for.
3. The very next step for them (e.g., 'Please check your inbox for an invitation to our client portal').
4. An introduction to their primary point of contact, [Account Manager Name].
5. A link to our general FAQ page: [Link to FAQ].
6. A clear call to action for any immediate questions.
Maintain a friendly yet professional tone."
```

**Explanation:** This prompt quickly generates a comprehensive welcome email, saving you valuable time on a critical first impression. Personalize with bracketed details to ensure relevance and a human touch.

---

**Prompt 2: Comprehensive Onboarding Checklist for Specific Service**

**Purpose:** Create a detailed, step-by-step onboarding checklist tailored to a specific service, ensuring no crucial step is missed.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Generate a comprehensive onboarding checklist for a new client engaging our [Specific Service, e.g., 'SEO Content Strategy'] service.
Include at least 15 steps covering:
1. Initial access requests (e.g., Google Analytics, CMS).
2. Key information gathering (e.g., brand guidelines, target audience).
3. Internal team assignments.
4. Setting up communication channels.
5. First strategic meeting preparation.
Organize steps logically with clear actions and responsible parties (Client/Agency)."
```

**Explanation:** This prompt allows you to quickly build a robust onboarding process for any service offering. It ensures consistency and clarity, reducing back-and-forth communication and client confusion.

---

**Prompt 3: Discovery Call Question Generator**

**Purpose:** Develop a list of insightful questions for a client discovery call to thoroughly understand their business, challenges, and goals.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Create a list of 10-12 open-ended discovery call questions for a potential client interested in our [Service Type, e.g., 'Social Media Management'] service.
Focus on understanding:
1. Their current challenges and pain points.
2. Their specific business goals and KPIs.
3. Their past experiences with similar services (if any).
4. Their ideal outcome from our service.
5. Budget and decision-making process.
Frame questions to encourage detailed responses, not just 'yes/no'."
```

**Explanation:** Preparing for discovery calls can be time-consuming. This prompt helps you generate a tailored set of questions that ensure you gather all necessary information to qualify leads and propose effective solutions.

---

**Prompt 4: Project Brief Template Outline**

**Purpose:** Draft a structured outline for a project brief, ensuring all essential information is captured before project commencement.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Outline a standard project brief template for a [Type of Project, e.g., 'Website Redesign Project'].
The template should include sections for:
1. Project Title & Overview.
2. Client Company Background.
3. Project Goals & Objectives (SMART criteria).
4. Target Audience.
5. Key Deliverables & Scope.
6. Timeline & Milestones.
7. Budget Allocation (if applicable).
8. Brand Guidelines & Preferred Tone.
9. Competitor Analysis (if provided).
10. Key Stakeholders & Approval Process.
11. Success Metrics.
Provide a brief description for what should be included in each section."
```

**Explanation:** A well-defined project brief is the foundation of a successful project. This prompt helps you create a comprehensive template that minimizes scope creep and ensures alignment between your agency and the client.

### Page 2

**Prompt 5: Client Portal Setup & Usage Guide**

**Purpose:** Generate clear, concise instructions for a client on how to access and effectively use your agency's client portal.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Write a step-by-step guide for a client on how to set up and use our client portal.
Assume the portal uses [Specific Platform, e.g., 'Dubsado' or 'ClickUp'].
Include instructions for:
1. Initial login and password setup.
2. Navigating key sections (e.g., 'Projects', 'Files', 'Invoices', 'Communication').
3. Uploading documents.
4. Viewing project progress.
5. Submitting feedback or questions.
Keep the language simple, user-friendly, and include tips for efficient use. Use markdown for clear steps."
```

**Explanation:** A clear guide for your client portal reduces support requests and empowers clients to self-serve. This prompt ensures your clients quickly become proficient with your chosen communication hub.

---

**Prompt 6: Monthly Client Report Outline**

**Purpose:** Create a structured outline for a monthly performance report, ensuring all critical data points and sections are covered.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Generate a detailed outline for a monthly client report for our [Service Type, e.g., 'Digital Marketing Campaign'].
The report should cater to a client who needs to understand campaign performance and ROI.
Include sections for:
1. Executive Summary (Key Highlights & Lowlights).
2. Campaign Overview & Goals Reminder.
3. Performance Metrics (e.g., Traffic, Conversions, Leads, Spend, ROI).
4. Analysis & Insights (What the numbers mean).
5. Key Activities Performed This Month.
6. Recommendations & Next Steps for the Upcoming Month.
7. Appendix for detailed data.
Suggest specific data points to include under 'Performance Metrics'."
```

**Explanation:** This prompt helps you standardize your reporting process. A consistent outline ensures that every report delivers comprehensive value, making it easier to prepare and for clients to understand.

---

**Prompt 7: Performance Metric Summary (from raw data)**

**Purpose:** Summarize raw performance data into a concise, easily digestible overview for a client report.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Summarize the following raw data into a 2-paragraph 'Performance Metrics Overview' section for a monthly client report for [Client Company Name]'s [Campaign Name].
Focus on key trends, achievements, and areas needing attention.

[Provide raw data here, e.g.,
'Website Traffic: Previous Month 10,000, Current Month 12,500 (+25%)'
'Conversion Rate: Previous Month 2.0%, Current Month 2.3% (+0.3pp)'
'Ad Spend: Previous Month $1,000, Current Month $1,200 (+20%)'
'Leads Generated: Previous Month 200, Current Month 280 (+40%)'
'Avg. Cost Per Lead: Previous Month $5.00, Current Month $4.28 (-14%)'
'Social Media Engagement: Previous Month 500, Current Month 750 (+50%)']

Highlight the most significant positive and negative changes."
```

**Explanation:** Instead of simply presenting numbers, this prompt helps you articulate the story behind the data. It saves time on analysis and ensures your client immediately grasps the most important performance aspects.

### Page 3

**Prompt 8: Actionable Insights & Recommendations**

**Purpose:** Translate performance data and observations into concrete, actionable insights and strategic recommendations for the client.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Based on the following observations and performance summary, generate 3-5 actionable insights and recommendations for [Client Company Name]'s [Campaign Name] monthly report.
Each insight should clearly state the implication, and each recommendation should be specific and forward-looking.

[Provide observations/summary here, e.g.,
'Observation 1: Blog traffic increased by 30%, but conversion rate from blog posts decreased by 0.5%.'
'Observation 2: Our top-performing ad creative achieved a 15% CTR, while others averaged 5%.'
'Observation 3: Email open rates are strong (35%), but click-through rates are low (3%).']

Focus on how these insights will drive client business goals."
```

**Explanation:** This prompt transforms raw data into strategic value. It helps you quickly identify opportunities and present clear next steps, positioning your agency as a proactive partner.

---

**Prompt 9: Next Steps & Future Projections**

**Purpose:** Draft a forward-looking section for a client report, outlining planned activities and realistic projections for the upcoming period.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Write a 'Next Steps & Future Projections' section for [Client Company Name]'s monthly report for the upcoming month.
Include:
1. A summary of 3-4 key activities planned based on current insights.
2. Expected outcomes or goals for these activities (e.g., 'Aim for a 10% increase in leads').
3. A brief explanation of *why* these steps are being taken.
4. A statement about ongoing optimization and strategy review.
Maintain a confident and results-oriented tone."
```

**Explanation:** This prompt helps you articulate your ongoing strategy and manage client expectations. It shows foresight and a clear path forward, reinforcing your agency's value.

---

**Prompt 10: Client Feedback Request Email (Post-Report)**

**Purpose:** Draft an email to the client, accompanying the monthly report, and explicitly inviting their feedback and discussion.

**The Prompt:**
```
"Draft an email to [Client Point of Contact Name] at [Client Company Name], accompanying their monthly [Service Type] report.
Include:
1. A polite introduction to the attached report.
2. A brief summary of 1-2 key highlights from the report.
3. A clear invitation for feedback and questions.
4. Suggest a follow-up call to discuss the report in more detail.
5. Reiterate commitment to their success.
Maintain a professional and collaborative tone."
```

**Explanation:** Following up with a report is crucial for client retention. This prompt ensures you send a proactive email that encourages engagement and opens the door for valuable feedback, strengthening client relationships.
